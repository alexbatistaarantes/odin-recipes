Vegan Cookie

The vegan cookie it's a delicious option for you that like to always have a little snack in your bag, or take rewards throught the day to boost your energy, or even if you want to sell it to make a few bucks. Suffice to say that they don't use any ingredient derived from animals.

This recipe will give you 4 cookies and take about 17 minutes to prepare. If you're intersted in, here are the ingredients, and the steps to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons vegan chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon almond milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Mix flour, chocolate chips, sugar, oil, cocoa powder, almond milk, vanilla extract, and baking powder together in a bowl. Place dough on a flat work surface and split into 4 pieces. Flatten and mold cookies into the desired shape. Arrange cookies on a small baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set, about 5 minutes. Cool until firm, 2 to 3 minutes.
